Who We Are

Giving a Voice to the Marginalized

TVMC is a youth-led, non-governmental, non-partisan, and non-profit organization. The organization was founded in 2011 by a group of youth volunteers with a vision to create a society that respects the rights of children and women. The organization was officially registered as "The Voice of Marginalized Community" in 2014 by the Ministry of Community Development, Gender and Children.
